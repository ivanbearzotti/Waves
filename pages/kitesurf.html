Kitesurf

Kitesurf is a sport that involves using wind power with a large power kite to pull a rider across a water, land or snow surface.

Jamie Overbeek

Red Bull KOTA World Champion

“It actually started on holiday in Denmark with a simple two-line kite when I was about six years old, just on the beach. When I was nine I took a landboarding course on Romo a peninsula in Denmark with a very large tidal beach, perfect for landboarding. That worked out well for me, the school saw something in me and made sure thar I came into contact with a sponsor, that was Fly Surfer. The same year I received kite usrfing lessons in Schellinkhout. I started with foil kites and after about two years tube kites were also added. Actually I like all disciplines, longboardingkiting, sepcialism big air and loops.”
